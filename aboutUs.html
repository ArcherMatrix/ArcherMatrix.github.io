    As a computer science graduate, there was thing I wished I had during my time as an undergrad: direction. I was learned many languages and designs but I didn't know what to do with them. This is where my idea, the Tinkering Interface Project, was created. Our goal is to give the new generation of computer scientist a direction as well as to connect them with experienced veterans.
